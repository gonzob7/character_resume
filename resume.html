Finn The Human

About:

Finn The Human is the main Protagonist in the american cartoon "Adventure Time"
Finn The Human lives in the post-apocalyptic Land of Ooo, which was ravaged by a cataclysmic nuclear war a thousand years before the series

Skills: Finn possesses a near-supernatural imagination that allows him to create anything, but only he can see his creations

Expierence: Destroyed a black hole with a fourth dimensional sword (although he cannot use this sword anymore). Considers hardcore dungeon crawling a hobby.

Achievements: Last Human alive in the land of Ooo.
